My Journey into Tech

From Curiosity into Coding

Meet Sarfowaa, a young woman who recently decided to embark on a new and exciting journey into the world of technology and coding. she had always been curious about how websites and applications
worked, but never imagine she would start learning how to build them herself

The First Steps

Sarfowaa began by learning the basics of HTML, the foundation of web development. She created her first web page, adding heading, paragraphs, and even formatting her text using bold, italics,
and underline elements.

Every new tag she learned brought a sense of accomplishment.
She quickly became comfortable with HTML elements and is eager to explore more.
Next on her list: learning more HTML elements and CSS to style her web pages!

Challenges and Growth

The journey wasn`t always easy. Sarfowaa encountered errors, bugs, and challenges that tested her patience. But she never gave up. She new that with every mistake, she was one step closer to becoming a
confident coder.

At the end of the program, Sarfowaa can build simple web pages and will slowly venture into Java Script to make her projects interactive.

The Road Ahead

Sarfowaa knows that the road to mastering tech is long, but she is excited for the future. She`s even started sharing her progress on social media to inspire others, especially women, to drive into the world of coding.

Conclusion

Sarfowaa`s journey is beginning. With passion, determination, and the ability to never stop learning and that the possibilities in tech are endless.
